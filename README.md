## ğŸš€ How to Use It

Once the app opens in your browser, just follow these steps:

1.  **Upload Audio:** Drag and drop your audio file (we support `.mp3`, `.wav`, or `.m4a`).
2.  **Pick your Settings:**
      * Type in the language code of your audio (like `hi` for Hindi).
      * Choose which "Brain" (Model) you want to use. You can pick the **Fast** one or the **Standard** one.
3.  **Hit Go:** Click the big **Start Processing** button.
4.  **Download:** Once it's done, you can see the text side-by-side and download a neat little report to share with others.
